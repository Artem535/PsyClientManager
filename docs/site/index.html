README

Practice management for client sessions

A native desktop planner for scheduling sessions, managing clients, tracking payments, and keeping a clear view of recurring work.

Read setup guide View changelog

Everything needed for daily scheduling

The app focuses on fast calendar work, client context, online session links, and lightweight analytics without a web account.

Calendar and timeline

Plan a day from the timeline, create sessions from empty slots, and keep work and regular events separated.

Recurring events

Create daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly series, choose weekdays, and edit a single session or future sessions.

Client workspace

Keep client details, quick actions, notes, and session history close to the scheduling flow.

Online sessions

Store external meeting links for Zoom, Meet, Telegram, or any other service, then open or copy invites quickly.

Designed around the appointment flow

PsyClientManager keeps common actions visible and avoids forcing scheduling work through heavy dialogs.

01

Plan

Pick a date, click the timeline, and create a session with client, payment, online link, and repeat settings.
